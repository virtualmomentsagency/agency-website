Common Inquiries

Experience the reveal: Hover or tap for answers

Do you accept Virtual Partners without experience?
Yes! Experience is a plus, but we believe in "can-do" attitudes, openness to knowledge, and strong work ethics above all.
Do you provide training for new partners?
Training is subject to the client's specific needs, but we provide 100% support along the way to handle any confusion.
What industries do you specialize in?
We primarily focus on Healthcare (Medspa/Aesthetics), B2B Sales, SaaS, and Real Estate industries.
How does the "Virtual Handshake" work?
This is a final video meeting where you meet your matched partner to confirm strategy and personal chemistry before starting.
Are there long-term contract lock-ins?
We believe in partnerships based on value. Our terms are flexible and designed to scale with your business growth.
How do you ensure the quality of work?
Through our multi-stage vetting process and continuous oversight from our Director of Operations.
Can I hire a partner for a part-time role?
Absolutely. We offer customized talent solutions ranging from part-time support to dedicated full-time virtual ecosystems.
Who handles the payroll and admin tasks?
VMA handles all back-office logistics, including payroll and compliance, so you can focus solely on your core operations.
What communication tools do you use?
We integrate with your preferred tools, typically Slack, WhatsApp Business, or professional email suites.
How do I start my journey as a Partner?
Simply use the "Become a Virtual Partner" button on our home page to access the application form and start vetting.
