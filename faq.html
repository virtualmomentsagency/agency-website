Common Inquiries

Do you accept Virtual Partners without experience?
Yes! Experience is a plus, but we value a "can-do" attitude, openness to new knowledge, and strong work ethics above all else.
Do you provide training for new partners?
Training is subject to the specific client role, but we provide 100% support along the way to ensure any confusion is quickly resolved.
What industries do you primarily support?
We specialize in Healthcare (Medspa/Aesthetics), B2B Sales, SaaS, and Real Estate industries.
How fast can I be matched with a Virtual Partner?
Our discovery and talent matching process typically takes 3 to 7 business days to ensure the perfect strategy alignment.
Are the Virtual Partners working full-time?
We offer both part-time and full-time arrangements tailored to the specific operational needs of your business.
How do we communicate with our Partner?
We encourage using professional tools like Slack, WhatsApp Business, or Email, depending on your preferred internal workflow.
What is the "Virtual Handshake"?
This is the final step where you meet your matched partner via video call to confirm chemistry and start the partnership.
Do you handle the payroll for the VAs?
Yes, VMA handles all administrative back-office tasks, including payroll, so you can focus solely on your business growth.
Is there a long-term contract requirement?
We maintain a select network of partners and offer flexible terms that focus on value and results rather than restrictive lock-ins.
How do you ensure the quality of work?
Through our multi-stage vetting process and ongoing operational support from our Director of Operations.
