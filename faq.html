Do you accept Virtual Partners without experience?
Yes! Experience is a plus, but we believe in a "can-do" attitude, openness to new knowledge, and strong work ethics above all else.
Do you provide trainings?
Training is subject to what the client is hiring for, but we provide 100% support along the way so we are always there to help.
What industries do you primarily support?
Healthcare (Medspa/Aesthetics), B2B Sales, SaaS, and Real Estate.
How fast can I be matched with a Partner?
Typically 3 to 7 business days to ensure perfect strategy alignment.
Are there long-term contract lock-ins?
We maintain a select network of partners and focus on value over restrictive lock-ins.
How do you ensure quality of work?
Multi-stage vetting and ongoing support from our Director of Operations.
Can I hire for a part-time role?
Yes. We offer customized talent solutions for both part-time and full-time needs.
Who handles the payroll and admin?
VMA handles all payroll and compliance, allowing you to focus on growth.
What communication tools do you use?
Slack, WhatsApp Business, or your preferred professional email suites.
What is the "Virtual Handshake"?
The final video call to meet your partner and confirm chemistry before starting.
