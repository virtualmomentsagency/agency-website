Do you accept Virtual Partners without experience?
Yes! Experience is a plus, but we believe in a "can-do" attitude, openness to new knowledge, and strong work ethics above all else.
Do you provide trainings?
Training is subject to what the client is hiring for, but we provide 100% support along the way so we are always there to help.
What industries do you primarily support?
We specialize in Healthcare (Medspa/Aesthetics), B2B Sales, SaaS, and Real Estate industries.
How fast can I be matched with a Partner?
Our discovery and talent matching process typically takes 3 to 7 business days to ensure perfect strategy alignment.
Are there long-term contract lock-ins?
We maintain a select network of partners and focus on value. Our terms are flexible and designed to scale with you.
How do you ensure the quality of work?
Through our multi-stage vetting process and ongoing operational support from our Director of Operations.
Can I hire a partner for a part-time role?
Absolutely. We offer customized talent solutions ranging from part-time support to dedicated full-time virtual ecosystems.
Who handles the payroll and admin?
VMA handles all back-office logistics, including payroll and compliance, so you can focus solely on growth.
What communication tools do you use?
We integrate with your preferred tools, typically Slack, WhatsApp Business, or professional email suites.
What is the "Virtual Handshake"?
The final step where you meet your matched partner via video call to confirm chemistry before starting the partnership.
