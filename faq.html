Yes! We value a "can-do" attitude and strong work ethics above all else.

Training is subject to role requirements, but we provide 100% operational support.

Healthcare (Medspa), B2B Sales, SaaS, and Real Estate.
How fast is the matching?
Typically 3-7 business days to ensure perfect strategy alignment.
Are there contract lock-ins?
We focus on value over restrictive lock-ins. Terms are flexible.

Multi-stage vetting and ongoing support from our Director of Operations.
Can I hire part-time?
Yes, we offer both part-time and dedicated full-time talent solutions.

VMA handles all payroll and compliance so you can focus on growth.
What tools do we use?
Slack, WhatsApp Business, and your existing professional suites.
What is the "Virtual Handshake"?
A final video call to meet your partner and confirm chemistry.
